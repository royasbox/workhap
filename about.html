About

Hiya, welcome to Work Hap. An affordable tool for those that desire a culture of innovation and creativity. Work Hap aims to simulate chance encounters in an office environment in the age of remote work.

Workplace happenstance!

Let's fight work fatigue by building meaningful relationships through chance encounters and unplanned interactions to stay happy, healthy, productive and engaged in the age of remote work.

No coordination needed

The impromptu nature of Work Hap interactions simulates how team members would run into each other in an office environment and removes the friction for coordinating meetings.

Create happenstance

Work Hap is designed to spark both collaboration and innovation that is triggered when people collide.

Increase productivity

These chance encounters will allows employees to enjoy time spent with like-minded colleagues while improving their performance and productivity.
